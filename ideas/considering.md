## Product
> 📄 **Leading recommendation now drafted** (research-backed, adversarially reviewed): [`../products/what-to-make-recommendation.md`](../products/what-to-make-recommendation.md). Awaiting your approval — summary below.
- **Hero = barrier-repair cream**, *not* a night serum. Working name **"Goldgrain Repair Cream"** — a ceramide + cholesterol + fatty-acid "Gold Seam Complex" + niacinamide; literally mending the skin's seams. (Research showed barrier-as-premium-hero is a real white-space gap; "another night serum" is dupe-bait.)
- **Launch lean (Phase 1 trio)**: Repair Cream (hero) + Goldwork Renewal Serum + Quiet Repair Cleanser — sold solo, as "The Mending Ritual" bundle, and as a minis trial trio; refill subscription live day one. Phase 2 adds AM vitamin-C defense, PM gentle retinoid renewal, eye. Phase 3: licensed longevity-peptide apex.
- **Signature longevity angle, told honestly**: niacinamide/NAD⁺ as *education*, GHK-Cu as "emerging" — never "senolytic." The strong-evidence ceramide barrier carries the efficacy promise.
- **Refillable / airless packaging** — "repair, don't discard"; also the LTV engine.
