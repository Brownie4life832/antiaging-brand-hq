# Voice & Tone

> **Status:** Working draft v0.1 (2026-06-18). Use this when writing any brand-facing copy. To formalize, run the `generate-guidelines` brand-voice skill against our best copy once we have some.

## Voice in one line

**A wise, warm friend who knows the science** — calm and self-respecting, never panicked, never shaming, never hypey.

## Personality

| We are | We are not |
|---|---|
| Calm, grounded | Urgent, fear-mongering |
| Warm, human | Cold, clinical-corporate |
| Confident, evidence-led | Boastful, miracle-claiming |
| Elegant, restrained | Loud, salesy, emoji-spammy |
| Honest about limits | Over-promising |
| Reverent about aging | Ageist, "anti-wrinkle warfare" |

## Tone shifts by context

- **Product pages:** confident + precise. Lead with the benefit and the *why* (the science), in plain language. Restrained.
- **Education / blog:** generous teacher. Demystify ingredients. Cite. No condescension.
- **Social:** warmer, a little more playful, still grounded. Beauty in the everyday.
- **Email:** personal, like a note from a knowledgeable friend.
- **Ads:** evocative + concrete. One idea, beautifully. Never desperate.

## Language rules

**Do**
- Speak *to* the reader as a peer ("you," "your skin").
- Use the kintsugi/gold/repair/renew lexicon naturally (see [`glossary.md`](glossary.md)).
- Name ingredients and explain what they do.
- Say what's proven — and admit what isn't.

**Don't**
- Use fear or shame ("hide your age," "fight wrinkles," "flaws").
- Make unsubstantiated or drug-like claims ("cures," "erases wrinkles," "boosts collagen 300%") — see claims guardrail in [`../CLAUDE.md`](../CLAUDE.md).
- Over-use exclamation points or hype words ("miracle," "revolutionary," "game-changer").
- Sound like every other clinical-white skincare brand.

## Signature words & phrases *(starter lexicon — grow this)*

- repair, renew, restore, mend, nourish, barrier, resilience
- gold, golden, gild, illuminate, story, history, grace
- "in gold," "mended," "honored," "made whole"

Avoid (overused category clichés): "glow up," "turn back the clock," "ageless," "fountain of youth," "anti-wrinkle."

## Quick before/after

- ❌ "Erase years of wrinkles with our miracle anti-aging serum!"
- ✅ "A renewal serum that helps repair your skin's barrier — so the years you've earned look like gold, not damage."
