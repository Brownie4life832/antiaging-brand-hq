## The core strategic question

**Do we position as "anti-aging" or reframe the category?**

| Framing | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| **Anti-aging** (conventional) | Huge, proven search demand; instantly understood; easy to market | Crowded, commoditized; fear-based; clashes with the kintsugi philosophy |
| **Pro-aging / graceful aging** | On-brand; differentiated; emotionally resonant; PR-friendly | Smaller existing demand; must educate the market; "feel-good" can read as soft on efficacy |
| **Longevity / skin-health** (science-forward) | Premium, credible, trend-aligned (longevity boom); claim-safe | Requires real science investment; less emotional |

**Recommended working stance:** Lead with the *kintsugi/pro-aging emotional story* on the surface, backed by *serious skin-longevity science* underneath — and still capture *anti-aging* search demand through SEO/paid (meet people where they're searching, convert them to our worldview). Decide and log in [`../decisions/decision-log.md`](../decisions/decision-log.md).

## Target audience *(hypotheses — validate)*

**Primary:** 35–55, predominantly women (with a deliberate door open to men/unisex), who are:
- Past the "fix every flaw" phase and want to *age well*, not *look 25*.
- Skeptical of hype; will read an ingredient list; influenced by derms/science creators.
- Willing to pay premium for fewer, better products with a story they believe in.

**Secondary:** 28–35 "prevention" buyers starting early; gift-buyers; the wellness/longevity crowd.

## Category & price tier *(draft)*

- **Category:** premium / "affordable luxury" clinical skincare. Not mass ($), not ultra-luxury ($$$$). Target the $$–$$$ "informed indulgence" tier (think the space around Paula's Choice ↔ Augustinus Bader, depending on positioning).
- **Channel:** DTC-first (own site / Shopify), later selective retail.

## Differentiation — what makes us not-a-commodity

1. **The kintsugi worldview** — emotional reframe nobody else owns.
2. **Repair-first formulations** — barrier, peptides, renewal actives; honesty about what works.
3. **Aesthetic** — gold/wabi-sabi premium design in a sea of clinical-white sameness.
4. **Radical claims honesty** — we say what's proven and what isn't.
