# Visual Identity

> **Status:** Direction / moodboard brief v0.1 (2026-06-18). This is a brief for design, not final assets. Use it to drive logo, packaging, site, and ad design (via Figma/Canva tools or a designer).

## Aesthetic north star

**Wabi-sabi luxury.** The beauty of imperfection, asymmetry, and age — rendered in premium, restrained design with gold as the hero accent. Think: a hand-thrown ceramic bowl mended in gold, photographed in warm natural light. The opposite of clinical-white, sterile, mass-market skincare.

## Color direction *(draft palette — to be refined in design)*

| Role | Direction | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Hero accent | **Gold** (warm, real-metal, not yellow) | The kintsugi gold. Use sparingly = premium. |
| Base / ground | Warm off-white / rice paper, soft clay | Calm, organic, not stark white |
| Ink / text | Deep charcoal / sumi black | High legibility, refined |
| Supporting | Muted ceramic tones — terracotta, sand, sage, deep plum | Earthy, mature, not pastel |

*Avoid:* neon, clinical blue/white, millennial pink, high-saturation gradients.

## Typography direction *(draft)*

- **Display/headline:** an elegant serif with character (literary, not corporate) — conveys heritage + craft.
- **Body/UI:** a clean, humanist sans — modern, legible, calm.
- Generous whitespace. Restraint. Let the gold and the imagery breathe.

## Logo / mark direction

- Wordmark "Kintsugi" in the display serif.
- Optional mark: a single golden seam/crack motif — a mended line — that can stand alone as an icon and repeat as a brand pattern.
- Works in gold-on-dark and ink-on-light.

## Imagery & art direction

- Warm, natural light. Real skin with texture and lines (no over-retouching — that *is* the brand).
- Kintsugi ceramics, gold seams, hands, ritual, calm.
- Diverse, mature models shown with dignity — beauty *with* age, not "after."
- Macro shots of textures (gold, cream, ceramic).

## Packaging direction *(draft)*

- Premium, tactile, refillable/sustainable where possible (on-brand: repair, not waste).
- Matte ceramic-feel bottles, gold detailing, the seam motif.

## Assets & tools
