# [BRAND] — Brand Headquarters

*(name / story / positioning OPEN)*

This repository is the **single source of truth and operating hub** for an anti-aging / skin-longevity skincare brand (name TBD). Everything about the brand lives here: identity, research, products, marketing, the website, and the running log of what we're considering (and what we've ruled out).

It is built to be **queryable** — ask Claude anything about the brand and the answer should be grounded in these files. As the brand evolves, this repo evolves with it.

The **brand name, story/metaphor, visual direction, and core positioning/worldview are all open and undecided.** Nothing here should assume a fixed name, narrative, or worldview; those are decisions still to be made. The durable foundation is the research base (ingredient evidence, market sizing and DTC economics, competitor facts, and the white-space gaps) and the product logic grounded in it.

## How this repo is organized

| Folder | What lives here |
|---|---|

| [`research/`](research/) | Ingredient science, market & competitor research, trends — the evidence base |

| [`decisions/`](decisions/) | The decision log — what we decided, when, and why |

## How to use it

- **Ask questions.** "What ingredients are we considering for the renewal serum?" "What's our position vs. [competitor]?" "Summarize what we've ruled out and why." Claude reads these files to answer.
- **Capture ideas as they come.** Drop anything into [`ideas/parking-lot.md`](ideas/parking-lot.md). We triage from there into *considering* or *not-considering*.
- **Do research.** Ask Claude to research an ingredient, competitor, or trend; results get written into [`research/`](research/) using the templates.
- **Make things.** Brand files, marketing copy, ad concepts, the website — all created and refined in place.
