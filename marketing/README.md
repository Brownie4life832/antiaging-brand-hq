# Marketing

Where the brand goes to market. All copy and creative should run through our [`../brand/voice-and-tone.md`](../brand/voice-and-tone.md) and respect the claims guardrail in [`../CLAUDE.md`](../CLAUDE.md).

## Folders

| Folder | What goes here |
|---|---|
| [`copy/`](copy/) | Core messaging, taglines, product-page copy, about page, FAQs |
| [`ads/`](ads/) | Ad concepts & copy (Meta, Google, TikTok, etc.) + creative briefs |
| [`campaigns/`](campaigns/) | Full campaign plans (launch, seasonal, promos) with calendars |
| [`social/`](social/) | Organic social content, content pillars, post drafts |
| [`email/`](email/) | Flows (welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase) and broadcasts |

## Skills to use

- **`/campaign-plan`** — full campaign brief: objectives, audience, channels, calendar, metrics.
- **`/content-creation`** or **`/draft-content`** — blog, social, email, landing pages, PR, case studies.
- **`/email-sequence`** — multi-email flows with timing, branching, benchmarks.
- **`/seo-audit`** — keyword & on-page strategy for the site/blog.
- **`/competitive-brief`** — positioning vs. competitors, messaging gaps.
- **`/brand-review`** — check any draft against our voice & claims before it ships.
- **`enforce-voice`** — apply our brand voice to any content automatically.
- **Canva / Figma MCP + image generation** — for ad and social creative.

## Working rule

Draft boldly, then **run it through `/brand-review`** for voice + claims. Anything that asserts an efficacy result needs a source in [`../research/`](../research/) or a `[needs substantiation]` flag.
