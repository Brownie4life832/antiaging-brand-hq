## ⚡ Flagged

- **Augustinus Bader goes *ingestible* — "The Skin" food-supplement line launches June 28 ($125 / 30 sachets).** Powered by a new "ERC8" complex; pitched as inside-out skin longevity targeting gut health, collagen synthesis, environmental defense and the skin's own oil production. AB cites a 12-week trial claiming +160% hydration / +180% firmness / +206% texture. **Why it matters for Kintsugi:** Coming days after the Dua Lipa "DUA" launch (yesterday's flag), AB is now **extending the longevity story into supplements** — a second front beyond topicals. Two strategic reads for us: (1) ingestible/"beauty-from-within" is a credible adjacency a lean topical brand can later bolt on; (2) those eye-popping % claims are exactly the substantiation tightrope we've committed *not* to walk — a useful contrast for our claims-discipline positioning. [Grazia](https://graziamagazine.com/us/articles/augustinus-bader-supplement-launch/) · [Moodie Davitt](https://moodiedavittreport.com/augustinus-bader-introduces-the-skin-food-supplement-line/) · [Zoe Report](https://www.thezoereport.com/beauty/augustinus-bader-the-skin-supplement)
- **Lancôme rolls out its Timeline-partnered topical longevity line — first Mitopure® (Urolithin A) in topical beauty, claiming to target skin's "biological age" via mitophagy.** A June 8 launch milestone building on the Feb partnership and the March AAD "Absolue Longevity MD" unveiling. **Why it matters:** A mass-prestige incumbent (L'Oréal-owned) is now planting a flag in *mechanistic* skin longevity — Urolithin A/mitophagy — the exact geroscience territory OneSkin and Timeline occupy. The premium-but-credible "skin longevity" lane is filling with deep-pocketed players fast; our differentiation needs to be sharper than "we do longevity too." Note the **"biological age" claim** edges toward structure/function — watch how a major handles the regulatory line. [GuruFocus](https://www.gurufocus.com/news/8904904/lancme-launches-longevity-skincare-line-amid-aging-concerns) · [NutraIngredients](https://www.nutraingredients.com/Article/2026/02/13/lancome-launches-topical-longevity-skincare-with-timeline/) · [Glossy](https://www.glossy.co/beauty/wellness/lancome-takes-longevity-to-dermatologists-raising-the-bar-for-beautys-buzziest-category/)

## Ingredients & Science

- **Senolytic "zombie-cell" drug ABT-263 reverses skin aging markers and speeds wound healing (preclinical).** New work (incl. a localized ABT-263 wound dressing in diabetic mice) reduced senescent-cell burden in aged skin and improved healing. **Claims flag — important:** ABT-263 is a *drug* (a senolytic), not a cosmetic ingredient; this is mouse/preclinical. It signals where "senotherapeutic skincare" hype is heading, but is the textbook example of a **drug claim we cannot make**. Useful for our science file, off-limits for copy. [ScienceDaily](https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/05/260519003215.htm)
- **Peptides for skin aging — 2026 systematic review & meta-analysis of RCTs (oral + topical).** Frontiers in Medicine published a pooled RCT analysis; a real evidence anchor for the barrier/collagen-support peptide story (vs. single small studies). Worth mining for which peptides have the cleanest human data before we shortlist actives. [Frontiers in Medicine](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/medicine/articles/10.3389/fmed.2026.1618306/full)
- **Bloodstream-bacterium metabolites (Paracoccus sanguinis) show anti-aging / anti-inflammatory activity in early research.** Indole-functionalized metabolites with antioxidant + antimicrobial effects — very early, speculative, but the kind of "novel microbial active" story suppliers will productize. Watch-only. [ScienceDaily roundup](https://www.thehealthy.com/news/anti-aging-molecules-study-january-2026/)

## Trends

- **Barrier-repair is the retail frame of the moment** — Sephora/Ulta merchandising is leaning into ceramides, lipids and barrier-repair complexes, reframing the category around *resilience and slow-aging* over aggressive correction. **Why it matters:** This is the clearest tailwind yet for our **premium barrier-repair cream** thesis — the market is actively teaching shoppers to value exactly what we'd build the brand on. [Marie Claire](https://www.marieclaire.com/beauty/skincare/best-korean-beauty-products-sephora/) · [Qogita](https://www.qogita.com/blog/tiktok-beauty-trends-2026/)
- **Hypochlorous acid surging — ~132% YoY search growth.** A barrier-/inflammation-friendly "calm skin" active going mainstream on TikTok. Worth tracking as a possible adjacent active or content hook (not a core SKU). [Qogita](https://www.qogita.com/blog/tiktok-beauty-trends-2026/)
- **Viral product watch:** a ginseng + retinal eye cream is the current TikTok "targets every sign of eye aging" breakout — note the *retinal* (not retinol) + botanical pairing pattern in viral actives. [Qogita](https://www.qogita.com/blog/tiktok-beauty-trends-2026/)

## Regulatory

- **EU scrutiny of "Sephora tween" marketing:** Italy's competition authority is investigating **Sephora and Benefit** over skincare marketed to children/tweens. **Why it matters:** Anti-aging actives marketed to minors is becoming a live regulatory + reputational risk across the category — a clear line for us to stay on the right side of (adult, evidence-led positioning; no minor-targeted anti-aging messaging). [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/27/sephora-benefit-investigation-skincare-marketing-children-tween-italy.html)
- **MoCRA grace period ending — systematic FDA enforcement began early 2026** against unregistered facilities / unlisted products; unauthorized drug claims + missing adverse-event contact info remain the top-cited violations. Reinforces yesterday's note: register early, get the boring compliance items right on day one. [Covington 2025 review](https://www.cov.com/en/news-and-insights/insights/2026/03/fda-advertising-and-promotion-enforcement-activities-2025-year-in-review) · [Cosmetics & Toiletries](https://www.cosmeticsandtoiletries.com/regulations/safety/news/22957642/fda-in-action-pfas-data-gaps-mocra-recall-guidance-warning-lettersrecalls-and-new-hair-growth-pill-reaches-phase-iii-trials)
