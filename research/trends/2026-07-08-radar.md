## Regulatory

- **Sharpened, new-to-radar: the 2026 FDA position on exosomes is now stated flatly — zero approved exosome products for aesthetic use, injectable or topical — and it triggers "the moment a product makes any structure-or-function claim."** Industry/legal coverage this window is explicit: "regenerates skin," "stimulates collagen," "reduces wrinkles" convert an exosome cosmetic into an unapproved drug regardless of delivery route. **Action for Kintsugi:** this is a clean guardrail to bank — if the exosome question is ever revisited (it's research-open, not adopted), the regulatory ceiling is low and the manufacturing QC is immature (see Ingredients). PDRN/polynucleotides are the comparatively lower-regulatory-risk regeneration-adjacent option, but still cannot carry structure/function language on a cosmetic label. [IAPAM — exosome therapy FDA regulations 2026](https://iapam.com/exosome-therapy-aesthetic-practice-fda-regulations) · [Unicorn Bioscience — exosome FDA status 2026: zero approvals](https://unicornbioscience.com/exosome-therapy-fda-status-2026/)

- **Standing FDA through-line unchanged:** the two most common warning-letter triggers remain (1) unauthorized drug claims — now increasingly evidenced from Instagram/TikTok/Amazon listings — and (2) missing MoCRA adverse-event contact info on-label. Premium price does not buy claim latitude; "clean beauty" therapeutic language is specifically watched. Design the first label with appearance/comfort claims only + MoCRA adverse-event contact from day one. [Foley & Lardner — how MoCRA is reshaping FDA oversight 2026](https://www.foley.com/insights/publications/2026/03/how-mocra-is-reshaping-fda-oversight-of-cosmetics-in-2026/) · [Crowell & Moring — trends in recent FDA cosmetic warning letters](https://www.crowell.com/en/insights/client-alerts/trends-in-recent-fda-warning-letters-for-cosmetic-and-cosmetic-adjacent-products)

- **EU clock, still running (flagged 07-03→07-07):** 56 new fragrance allergens mandatory-label from **Jul 31**, updated INCI Glossary mandatory from **Jul 30** (now ~3 weeks out), PFAS REACH cutoff **Oct 10, 2026**. Design the first label EU-ready. [COSlaw — allergen FAQ](https://coslaw.eu/fragrance-allergens-july-eu-deadline-faq/)
