# Evidence Matrix — Master synthesis of all ingredient dossiers

**Compiled:** 2026-06-19 · **Source:** the per-ingredient dossiers in this folder (read directly from disk). · **Dossiers covered:** **269** (every `*.md` in `research/ingredients/` except `_`-prefixed index/template files).

> **How to read this.** One row per dossier. Rows are sorted **strongest-evidence first** (A → F). The **Evidence grade** is each dossier's *adversarial* overall grade for the **brand-relevant claim** (topical, leave-on, anti-aging / skin-longevity) — i.e. when a dossier splits its grade ("A for acne, D for anti-aging"), the **anti-aging-efficacy** grade is used here, not the off-target one. **Top evidence tier** = the strongest tier the dossier actually credits for that claim. **Topical viability** = whether it can realistically go in a cosmetic (vs. a drug / research-chemical / delivery-blocked molecule). **Verdict** is the dossier's brand recommendation: ✅ shortlist/hero-capable · ⚠️ conditional/supporting · ❌ skip.

**Grade scale (as used across the dossiers):** **A** = strong human-topical RCT evidence · **B** = moderate, real human-topical (thinner/sponsored/combination) · **C** = weak / indirect / mechanism-led · **D** = unproven topically (in-vitro/animal/oral-only or borrowed) · **F** = no relevant evidence at all (for the anti-aging claim). +/− are within-band steps.
