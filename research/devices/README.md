# Research — Devices & Procedures

The evidence base for **modalities beyond topical actives**: energy-based devices, mechanical and
injectable procedures, biologics, and systemic ("wellness") treatments that consumers use for skin
aging. This is the device/procedure parallel to [`../ingredients/`](../ingredients/) — same bar, same
honesty standard, same controlled hallmark vocabulary so the two libraries cross-reference cleanly.

> **Scope note.** "Devices" is shorthand. This folder covers the full landscape: at-home energy
> devices (LED, microcurrent, RF), device-driven procedures (microneedling, microinfusion, lasers,
> peels), injectables & biologics (toxin, fillers, PRP, polynucleotides, exosomes, stem cells), and
> systemic treatments (IV therapy). Some are home/consumer, some are clinic/medical — each dossier
> flags **at-home vs in-clinic** and the **regulatory class** (device vs drug vs cosmetic).

## Why this vertical exists

Three jobs, in order of weight for a premium *topical* skincare brand:

1. **Education & credibility.** "Does red light actually work? Is microcurrent real? Are at-home
   microneedling pens safe?" are top consumer questions. Honest, tiered evidence powers SEO/content
   and the brand's science-honesty positioning.
2. **Product adjacency.** At-home LED, microcurrent, and microinfusion are large DTC categories. This
   library is the diligence base for any future device SKU, device+serum kit, or partnership.
3. **Competitive & category context.** Where topicals win, complement, or lose vs. devices and clinic
   procedures — and how rivals (Dr Dennis Gross, Angela Caglia, AnteAge, Plated, Rejuran, etc.) use
   them. See [`../competitors/`](../competitors/).

## How to do research here

- **Same standard as ingredients.** Exhaustive, citation-backed, **evidence separated strictly by
  tier** (mechanistic/in-vitro → animal → human: RCT → controlled → open-label → case). Real source
  URLs. No source = mark `[unverified]`.
- **Use the [`_template.md`](_template.md).** Copy it to `<slug>.md` and fill every section.
- **Map to the 11 hallmarks** (collagen/ECM, barrier, oxidative stress, glycation, senescence,
  mitochondrial, inflammaging, photoaging, pigmentation/tone, microbiome, hydration) — the same
  controlled vocabulary as the ingredient base.
- **Regulatory line matters more here.** Energy devices are often **FDA-cleared (510(k)) devices** or
  **OTC** — a different regime from cosmetics. Injectables/biologics can be **drugs/biologics** or
  **unapproved**. Flag the class and what a *cosmetic brand* could legally claim or sell.
- **End with a brand verdict.** Product-adjacency call (sell / bundle / partner / context-only /
  avoid) + how the modality interacts with our topical barrier-repair thesis (competes vs complements).
- **Add an adversarial verification pass** at the bottom (overall evidence grade A–F + citation audit
  + caveats), exactly like the ingredient dossiers.
- **Feed decisions back** to [`../../ideas/`](../../ideas/) and
  [`../../decisions/decision-log.md`](../../decisions/decision-log.md) when research settles something.
