# Product Roadmap

> **Status:** Working draft v0.1 (2026-06-18). All speculative — nothing decided. A starting point to react to.

## Strategy

Launch with a **tight hero range** that tells the repair/renew story end-to-end, rather than a sprawling line. A classic lean anti-aging routine is **cleanse → treat (serum) → moisturize → protect (SPF)**. A strong, ownable hero is often a **night renewal serum** (the "gold" / repair-while-you-rest story writes itself).

## Build vs. buy

Decide early — drives everything downstream:
- **Private label / white label** — fastest, cheapest, least differentiated.
- **Custom formulation via contract manufacturer** — slower/pricier, ownable, on-brand for "honest formulations."
- Research this in [`../research/science/`](../research/science/) and decide in [`../decisions/decision-log.md`](../decisions/decision-log.md).
