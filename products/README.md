# Products

The product line — from concept to formulation to roadmap.

- [`roadmap.md`](roadmap.md) — what we're launching, in what order
- [`concepts/`](concepts/) — one file per product concept. Use [`concepts/_template.md`](concepts/_template.md).
- [`formulations/`](formulations/) — ingredient decks / formula briefs per product (feeds a contract manufacturer or chemist)

## Principle

**Fewer, better.** A tight hero range that each earns its place beats a sprawling catalog. Every product should map to the "repair / renew" thesis and be backed by [`../research/ingredients/`](../research/ingredients/).

A concept graduates to a formulation only once its hero ingredients have research files and a clear job-to-be-done.
